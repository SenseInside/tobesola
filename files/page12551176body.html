Engineering stuff
Services
Is the capsule a 3D prototype?
No. The overland capsule is neither image nor prototype; these are real construction and engineering solutions that can you can visit and see on your own.

The overwater version is currently in pilot production.
What is the size of the capsule?
A four-person overland capsule is 3.7 × 12.2 × 3.7 meters, with 40 sq.m. of floor space.

Fully equipped capsule weighs 20 tons
What construction technology do you use?
The capsule is constructed using wood frame prefab technology (i.e. manufactured and equipped in the plant); the frame is filled with hard foam (BASF polyurethane foam, 60 kg / m3)
Is the capsule safe?
Yes, the capsule is absolutely safe.

All our locations are surveilled 24/7, but you will barely notice it.

The capsule features anti-vandal façade and can be locked or opened remotely through a smartphone.

Panoramic video cameras with images displayed on a mirror or a smartphone.

GPS-recognition of the owner, numerous sensors and video cameras, fingerprint opening.

The capsule features a fall sensor that will notify an authorized person of an accident
Where are the capsules produced?
The capsule has been developed and is being assembled by Ukrainian engineers on a Ukrainian plant. Pilot production is conducted in Kyiv. The assembly line production plant is being constructed in larger Kyiv area
How can I order a capsule?
E-mail us capsule@tobesola.com or call us +38 044 390 11 44. Our manager will give you all details
What kind of doors and windows are installed in the capsule?
It features an expandable Shucco system sized 2.7 × 4.6 m with a triple-pane window (with a 14-mm warm spacer frame) of three 6-mm and 8-mm heat-saving glasses and 526 kgs of weight.

The doors are by Reiner.

The rooftop window is 800 × 1,750 mm, 100 kg, and with a built-in four-pane window with PDLC glaze allowing to regulate the transparency of the glass
What home appliances is the capsule equipped with?
The capsule is equipped with Miele home appliances. It features a cooking stove, a bake oven, a coffee machine with cappuccinatore, a refrigerator, a dishwasher, a washing and drying machine, a multimedia screen, and a vacuum-cleaning robot
How is the capsule autonomous?
The capsule operates with its own electric power. It features walkable rooftop & sides thin-film Solibro sun panels with total capacity of 3.77 kW operating even on cloudy days (this type of panels boasts a 20-percent higher efficiency factor compared to polycrystal and monocrystal ones).

The capsule features a 10-kW storage battery that provides full operability of the house's systems during up to two days. The storage battery takes three hours of a sunny day to get fully charged.

Water autonomy is also planned for the capsule, to be provided with a 1,000-liter water tank. This option will be additionally supported with the system of gathering rainwater and ice water, the system of water reuse (shower, dishwasher, etc.), and the system of producing water from reservoirs and air
Will the capsule operate autonomously on cloudy days?
Yes, the featured system of solar panels allows generating electricity during cloudy weather as well
Is there a reserve mechanism to support major functions of the capsule?
Yes. The capsule features a 5kW diesel generator
How do you get fresh air inside the capsule?
The capsule is equipped with an air-cleaning system (ventilation with recuperation, air humidifying system, air disinfecting system) with automated control of CO2 and humidity levels
How is the capsule heated and cooled?
The water in the capsule is heated and cooled down with a water-air heat pump, with capacities of 5kW for cooling and 6kW for heating. Its energy conversion efficiency is 4.11. The floor is water-heated
How is the hammam special?
The hammam for two is printed on our own 3D printer from recycled plastic. The benches are water-heated and boast ergonomic shape repeating the shape of your body
What is a roboarm and what is it for?
The roboarm is a robotized mechanism navigating the location of the multimedia screen; the screen might be directed at living room, bedroom, kitchen, terrace, or parking lot
What is the warranty period for the capsule?
Capsule framing — 50 years.

Utility systems — 5 years.

Outside surfaces — 10 years.

Doors and windows — as provided by manufacturer.

Furniture — 10 years.

Home appliances — as provided by manufacturer
Is the capsule customizable?
Yes. It features several customizable areas:

  1. Overland or overwater.

  2. Exterior: material and color.

  3. Interior: material and color.

  4. Number of seats: two or four.

  5. Technical features: adding or removing any technical features from the catalog
What are delivery dates for the capsule?
Delivery dates within Ukraine are several days after loading ex-works.

Global delivery dates depend on the distance and shipping method.

Regular loading ex-works is planned for 2021
Do you offer global delivery?
Yes!
Do you provide access to those picturesque and uncrowded locations?
Yes! Our bank of unique locations is available to you*. These include proper rivers and lakes, beaches, forests, subalpine meadows, mountains. No plants, highways, people. Clean and open, free, and spacious.

*At extra cost

Two relocations anywhere within Ukraine are included in the price. Each subsequent relocation will cost about $2,000 (plus actual shuttle car mileage)

If you have any questions on how to use the capsule within the warranty period, our service company will answer them or assist you with them. It can be done both remotely (all the capsule systems can be navigated remotely) and on the spot, if needed
